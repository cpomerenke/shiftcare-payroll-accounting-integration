System Integration Overview

Employment Hero Payroll ↔ ShiftCare ↔ Accounting Platform

Data flows between the payroll management, care management, and accounting platforms

Integration powered by ShiftCare
Employment & payroll management platform
Employee Onboarding
  • Employees invited & onboarded here
  • Bank, tax & super details captured
  • Employee profiles pushed to ShiftCare
Payroll Data Stored
  • Pay rates & SCHADS / NDIS award rules
  • Work types, allowances & shift loadings
  • Leave balances & entitlements
  • Tax, super & STP obligations
Pay Run Processing
  • Timesheets ingested from ShiftCare
  • Shift activities & leave applied
  • SCHADS award auto-interpreted on import
  • Pay calculated & payslips issued
  • ATO STP reporting & compliance
Intentional Boundaries
  • No NDIS claim data received from ShiftCare
  • No post-payroll sync back to ShiftCare
  • Invoice & billing data stays in ShiftCare
💡 Payroll is the source of truth for employee entitlements, tax obligations, and pay calculations. ShiftCare feeds the hours — EH does the maths.
